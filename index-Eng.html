Chapter II

War in Abkhazia

The armed conict in Abkhazia started in 1992.

Fighting between Georgian government troops and
part of separatist ethnic Abkhazians supported by the
Armenian Battalion of Bagramian, the North Cauca-
sian mercenaries and the Cossack armed forces lasted
for 13 months and 13 days.

The separatists were unocially backed by the Rus-
sian military base garrison in Gudauta.

During the conict, numerous military oenses have
been committed, including ethnic cleansing of
Georgians. During the war, tens of thousands of
people were killed from both sides.

According to the Red Cross study conducted in 1999,
90 percent of Abkhazians and 42 percent of Georgians
say they have experienced ‘negative eects’ of the
conict, including the killing or rape of relatives, the
looting or destruction of homes, or being taken as a
prisoner.
